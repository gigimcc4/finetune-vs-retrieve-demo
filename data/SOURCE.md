# Corpus provenance

**File:** `TM-5-3805-237-35_grader.pdf`
**Document:** TM 5-3805-237-35 — Direct Support, General Support, and Depot
Maintenance Manual, Grader, Road, Motorized (diesel, 12 ft blade). U.S. Army.
**Pages:** 385
**Source:** Internet Archive military technical manual collection.

**License:** Works prepared by an officer or employee of the U.S. federal
government as part of their official duties are in the **public domain** under
17 U.S.C. § 105. This manual can be redistributed in this repository.

**Why this document:** It is (1) uncontaminated — the base LLM has not memorized
its specs, so a correct answer must come from what we add; and (2) universally
relatable — a maintenance manual is the shape of nearly every company's internal
ops/SOP documentation. Swap it for your own manual and the method is unchanged.

To use your own corpus, drop a PDF in this folder, point `src/build_corpus.py`
at it, and re-run the pipeline.
